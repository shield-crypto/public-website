FAQ

What is the minimum stake amount?
There is no minimum stake amount, but we recommend at least 100 NAM for meaningful rewards.
What is Shield Crypto's commission rate?
We maintain a competitive 10% commission rate to maximize returns for our delegators while ensuring sustainable operations.
How can I start staking with Shield Crypto?
Simply acquire NAM tokens and delegate them to our validator address. Check our guide section for detailed instructions.
What security measures does Shield Crypto implement?
We use enterprise-grade hardware, HSM key management, DDoS protection, and maintain multiple backup systems for maximum security and uptime.
Do you share some metrics about your validator?
We aim to have highest transparency and provide metrics about our validator through a public Grafana dashboard.
